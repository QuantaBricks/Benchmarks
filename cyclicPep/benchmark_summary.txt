           环肽构象相对能量相关性基准测试

─── 测试背景 / Background ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────

本测试针对10个环肽体系，评估两种半经验量子化学方法（GFN2-xTB 和 g-xTB）
对分子动力学（MD）采样构象集合的相对能量描述能力，以 MD 轨迹中记录的
Gasteiger 力场能量（单位：eV）作为参考。

This benchmark evaluates the ability of two semiempirical quantum chemistry
methods — GFN2-xTB and g-xTB — to reproduce the relative conformational
energies of cyclic peptide ensembles generated by molecular dynamics (MD)
sampling. Gasteiger force-field energies recorded in the MD trajectory XYZ
files (in eV) serve as the reference.

─── 测试体系 / Systems ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

共10个环肽体系，分子量涵盖77–117个重原子，包含中性（charge=0）和
带电体系（charge=−1, −2）。每个体系从 MD 轨迹中等间隔抽取 200 个构象。

and charged variants (charge = 0, −1, −2). For each system, 200 conformers
were evenly sampled from MD trajectories.

─── 计算方法 / Computational Protocol ──────────────────────────────────────────

1. 单点能计算（不做几何优化）：
   保持 MD 构象的原始几何，分别用 GFN2-xTB 和 g-xTB 做单点计算，
   以确保测试的是两种方法对同一能量面的描述，而非优化后构型的差异。

   Single-point energy calculations (no geometry optimization):
   All 200 conformers per system were computed at their MD geometries using
   both GFN2-xTB and g-xTB, ensuring a direct comparison of potential energy
   surface descriptions without relaxation artifacts.

2. 软件版本：
   GFN2-xTB : xtb 6.7.1 (Grimme group, University of Bonn)
   g-xTB    : xtb 6.7.1 + g-xTB v2.0.1 (--gxtb flag, via tblite)
   参考能量  : Gasteiger 部分电荷方法，由 MD 轨迹直接读取，单位 eV

   Software:
   GFN2-xTB : xtb 6.7.1
   g-xTB    : xtb 6.7.1 with g-xTB v2.0.1 (--gxtb, via tblite)
   Reference : Gasteiger partial-charge energies from MD trajectory (eV)

3. 相对能量转换：
   各方法独立以最低能构象为零点，转换为相对能量（kcal/mol）：
     ΔE_ref  = (E_ref  − min(E_ref))  × 23.0605  [eV → kcal/mol]
     ΔE_GFN2 = (E_GFN2 − min(E_GFN2)) × 627.509  [Hartree → kcal/mol]
     ΔE_gxTB = (E_gxTB − min(E_gxTB)) × 627.509  [Hartree → kcal/mol]

   Relative energies are computed independently for each method by
   subtracting the minimum energy in the ensemble:
     ΔE_ref  = (E_ref  − min) × 23.0605  [eV → kcal/mol]
     ΔE_GFN2 = (E_GFN2 − min) × 627.509  [Hartree → kcal/mol]
     ΔE_gxTB = (E_gxTB − min) × 627.509  [Hartree → kcal/mol]

4. 相关性指标：
   Pearson r（线性相关）、Spearman ρ（排序相关）、RMSE、MAE（kcal/mol）

   Metrics: Pearson r (linear), Spearman ρ (rank), RMSE, MAE (kcal/mol)

─── 结论 / Conclusions ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. 两种方法与参考能量的相关性均很高（Pearson r > 0.93），说明
   GFN2-xTB 和 g-xTB 都能合理重现 MD 采样给出的构象能量排序。

   Both methods show high correlation with the reference energies
   (Pearson r > 0.93), indicating that both GFN2-xTB and g-xTB
   reliably reproduce the conformational energy ranking from MD sampling.

2. g-xTB 在9/10个体系中 Pearson r 均高于 GFN2-xTB，平均提升约 0.013；
   RMSE 平均降低约 1.1 kcal/mol（4.67 → 3.60 kcal/mol），
   说明 g-xTB 对环肽构象能量面的描述整体优于 GFN2-xTB。

   g-xTB outperforms GFN2-xTB in 9 out of 10 systems: mean Pearson r
   improves by ~0.013 and mean RMSE decreases by ~1.1 kcal/mol
   (4.67 → 3.60 kcal/mol), indicating better description of the
   conformational energy landscape for cyclic peptides.

3. 带电体系（01: q=−1，04: q=−2）中两种方法的差距更为明显，
   g-xTB 的改进幅度（RMSE 减少 0.8–1.3 kcal/mol）大于中性体系，
   说明 g-xTB 在处理带电分子时有更显著的优势。

   The improvement of g-xTB over GFN2-xTB is more pronounced for
   charged systems (01: q=−1, 04: q=−2), with RMSE reductions of
   0.8–1.3 kcal/mol, suggesting g-xTB better handles electrostatics
   in charged cyclic peptides.

4. 体系06（q=0，89原子）两种方法的 RMSE 均偏大（~6 kcal/mol），
   提示该体系的 MD 力场能量面与半经验 QM 有较大系统性偏差，
   可能与该分子的特殊官能团或构象柔性有关，值得进一步分析。

   System 06 shows relatively large RMSE (~6 kcal/mol) for both
   methods, suggesting a systematic discrepancy between the MD
   force field and semiempirical QM for this particular system,
   possibly due to specific functional groups or unusual conformational
   flexibility. Further investigation is warranted.
